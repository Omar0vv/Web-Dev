Borsch

  • Time: 2hrs
Ingredients Quantity
Meat (either pulp with a little fat, or marrow bone with meat) 400 gt
Potatoes (depending on the pan. I have a medium one) 5
Beets 3
Garlic 1
Carrots 3
White cabbage / Cabbage 1 fork
Tomato (large) 1
Vinegar 2 tsp
Sugar 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Dill 1 tsp

Step 1: Put the meat to boil.

Step 2:When the meat is cooked, add the peeled potatoes

Step 3:Minutes after 5 add shredded cabbage. The lid is not completely covered.

Step 4:Heat up a frying pan and add a little vegetable oil. Put grated carrots and beets on a coarse grater. Immediately pour 2 tbsp. l. vinegar (if essence is used, then it must be diluted) and 1 tbsp. l. Sahara. Stir, cover and let simmer for 15 minutes. IMPORTANT! Just stew, not fry. If the beets are not very juicy, add a couple of tablespoons of broth. At the end, put the grated tomato on a grater.

Step 5:When the cabbage and potatoes are almost ready, add the stewed carrots and beets to them. Mix everything. Leave to simmer on a VERY SLOW fire to saturate.

THE MOST IMPORTANT THING IS NOT TO BOIL BORSHCH, OTHERWISE THE TASTE AND COLOR ARE LOST.

Step 6: Sprinkle the finished borscht with chopped dill and garlic. Cover with a lid and let it brew a little more.
